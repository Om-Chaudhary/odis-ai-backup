# Quick Start

This guide will help you make your first AI-powered call in under 5 minutes.

## Prerequisites

- Completed [installation steps](/getting-started/installation)
- Access to a phone to test calls

## Making Your First Test Call

### 1. Open the Dashboard

Navigate to [odis.ai/dashboard](https://odis.ai/dashboard) and log in.

### 2. Go to Test Mode

Click **Test Call** in the top navigation bar. This opens the testing interface where you can simulate calls without affecting your live system.

### 3. Initiate a Test Call

1. Enter your phone number in the test interface
2. Click **Start Test Call**
3. Answer your phone when it rings
4. Have a natural conversation with your AI agent

### 4. Review the Call

After the call ends:

1. View the transcript in your dashboard
2. Check the call summary and any actions taken
3. Review sentiment analysis and key topics

## Example Conversations

### Inbound Call (Appointment Scheduling)

```
Agent: "Thank you for calling Oakwood Veterinary Clinic. This is ODIS,
        your AI assistant. How can I help you today?"

Caller: "Hi, I'd like to schedule a checkup for my dog."

Agent: "I'd be happy to help you schedule a checkup. May I have your
        name and your dog's name?"
```

### Discharge Follow-up Call

```
Agent: "Hi, this is ODIS calling from Oakwood Veterinary Clinic.
        I'm following up on Bella's visit yesterday. Is this a good time?"

Owner: "Yes, what's this about?"

Agent: "I wanted to check how Bella is doing after her procedure
        and remind you about her medication schedule."
```
