# Installation

Get started with ODIS AI by setting up your account and configuring your first voice agent.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have:

- An ODIS AI account ([sign up here](https://odis.ai/signup))
- Access to your clinic's phone system
- Administrator access to your practice management software (optional, for integrations)

## Step 1: Create Your Account

1. Visit [odis.ai](https://odis.ai) and click **Get Started**
2. Enter your clinic information
3. Verify your email address
4. Complete the onboarding questionnaire

## Step 2: Configure Your Phone Number

ODIS AI can work with your existing phone system or provide a dedicated number:

### Option A: Use Our Provided Number

We'll provision a local phone number that forwards to your AI agent.

### Option B: Forward Your Existing Number

Configure call forwarding from your current number to ODIS AI during after-hours or overflow.

3. Choose a template (Inbound Support, Discharge Calls, etc.)
4. Customize the agent's personality and responses
5. Test with a sample call
